Prof. Janez Stare, though he is no stranger to Nuclear medicine is probably one of the most exciting faculty that we have on board for this meeting. An exceptionally articulate man, who is a gifted orator, we would like to introduce him as Professor at the Institute of Biostatistics and Medical Informatics at Ljubljana, Slovenia. He graduated in 1977 at University of Ljubljana, Faculty of Mathematics, field of Mathematics. Thesis: "Group of units in the field of algebraic numbers". He completed his Master degree 1989 at University of Ljubljana, Faculty of Medicine, field of Biostatistics. Master degree thesis: "Statistical analysis of survival time and its application in survival after myocardial infarction", Ph.D. 1994 at University of Ljubljana, Faculty of Medicine, field of Biostatistics. Thesis: "Measures of explained variation in survival analysis" (in English).

He worked as a Research fellow, Teaching assistant, Assistant professor, Head of the Department and Associate professor at Institute of Biostatistics and Medical Informatics.
